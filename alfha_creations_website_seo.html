SEO search engine optimization

Standing for “search engine optimization.” In layman terms, seo means the process of improving your website, making changes to it to increase its visibility for searches related to your services The better visibility your pages have in search results, higher the chance you are to attract attention and attract prospective and existing customers to your business.
